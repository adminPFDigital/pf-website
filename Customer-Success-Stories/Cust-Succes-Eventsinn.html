Events Inn


About Events Inn :


The customer is based out of UK and specializes in Events management, wanting to expand their operations in Asia. The associates of the company use manual tools such as MS-Excel to track each of the customers. PF Digital provided them with a lightning based solution on Salesforce


Scope Of The Project :


  • Customer Registration via Portal
  • Products selected for an Event
  • Event Planning
  • A Quote Generation
  • Online Payments
  • Location Rental, Location assignment to Vendors

Challenges :


  • Lightning components for Product selection
  • Drag n Drop of vendors to the location for allocation

    • Solution Implemented :


  • Salesforce community for customer to register and choose events
  • Community shopping cart to pick and choose between the products across different categories
  • Salesforce Lightning application to view the upcoming events, past events, the vendors for all those products
  • Eligible Vendor information based on criteria for selection
  • Single interface to upload invitees and send invites automatically using a template

    • Technology Stack :


    Sales Force, Visual Force Pages, Apex
    Lightning App
